What We Do

IMPACT Technology Solutions LLC is a leading provider of video conferencing solutions targeted to the judicial, education, and commercial markets. Videoconferencing is our core business and we are committed to providing turnkey solutions including video-arraignment, distance learning, video hearings, and video field trips, designed to create the greatest impact for our clients, supported with exceptional service at competitive prices.

Video Arraignment

Improve safety and reduce costs for your county or municipality with our video arraignment solution. See how other local governments have been able to increase security in the courtroom, and on their streets with this technology without increasing costs. We can assess your courtroom or jail and recommend a cost effective solution to meet your needs.
IMPACT Technology Solutions provides total turnkey support from installation to post warranty service including a single point of contact for hardware or network issues.

Distance Learning

Enhance your student's learning environment through the use of distance learning. Take video field trips to hundreds of sites and introduce subject matter experts to your class without ever leaving your building! IMPACT Technology Solutions can assist you in making distance learning a successful tool to improve the educational experience in your school. From planning to installation and ongoing support and maintenance, ITS will project manage all aspects of your installation and provide in-service training.

Video Conferencing

Improve your organization's productivity and reduce costs with videoconferencing. Not only can travel be reduced, this technology can enable your organization to increase productivity by allowing more frequent face to face communication. It is easy to share PC content including presentations, project plans, product development info, and training material with local and remote meeting participants. If you haven't experienced a videoconference lately, you will be surprised at the quality enhancements and lower price compared with just a few years ago. We will be happy to design a solution that meets your organization's needs and is suitable for your environment.

Network Support

IMPACT Technology Solutions provides voice, data, and video network support services to clients as a stand alone service or as part of a videoconferencing installation. We can help you to deploy the most cost effective network to support your specific needs. Services include local, long distance, POTS, ISDN, T1, Frame Relay, Managed Ethernet, and DSL, for voice, video and data applications.
